About

A game engineer with a research edge.

I’m currently pursuing a Master of Entertainment Arts and Engineering in Game Engineering at the University of Utah. I like building systems where gameplay, AI, and real-time interaction all meet: RL agents in Minecraft, adaptive controllers in VR, NPC behavior in Unreal, and hardware-linked interactive experiences.

I care about responsive feel, readable code, experimentation, and systems that can survive contact with actual players. That means I’m just as interested in behavior design and iteration as I am in the underlying technical stack.

What I work on

  • Game AI, reinforcement learning, and agent behavior
  • XR/VR interactions and hardware-integrated prototypes
  • Simulation and experimental interactive systems
